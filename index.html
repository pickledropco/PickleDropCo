The Process

How It Works

Three steps. Zero hassle. One unforgettable event.

01

You Book

Tell us about your event, venue, and group. We confirm your space qualifies and send a custom quote. No surprises, no commitment until you're ready.

02

We Show Up

On event day, we deliver and set up a full-size regulation court at your venue. We handle all on-site logistics. You do absolutely nothing.

03

You Play

Your guests enjoy a professional pickleball experience. When it's over, we pack up and disappear. You take all the credit. That's the whole point.
